About

I’m Chris Ross Harris, a designer and photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky specializing in branding and visual design systems using technologies like Figma, Adobe, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I’m working at Leap Group Network as a Visual Designer.

During my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with renowned brands such as WORK Labs. White Stone Oyster Co. and L.C. King Mfg. Co..
