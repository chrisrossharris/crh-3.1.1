About

I’m Chris Ross Harris, a designer, front-end developer and photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky specializing in crafting innovative digital experiences. With expertise in front-end development I blend creativity and technology to build interactive apps, websites, and tools, incorporating AI-driven solutions, custom branding, and seamless user experiences.

My projects range from AI-powered marketing platforms to intuitive interfaces using technologies like JavaScript, Tailwind, and Astro. Whether designing brand strategies or coding dynamic websites, I focus on delivering impactful results for clients and enhancing their digital presence.
